Retirement Projection

See what your pension can grow into.

Enter your current balance, expected yearly return, private monthly investing, employer or state monthly pension payments, years until retirement, and expected years in retirement. The calculator compounds monthly, can include annual fees, estimates retirement payouts after tax, and keeps the remaining money growing while you draw it down.

Model assumptions Growth is converted from annual return into an equivalent monthly rate and reduced by the annual fee drag. Private and employer/state monthly contributions are added at the end of each month. Private and employer/state contributions can each have their own yearly increase rate. Retirement withdrawals are taken monthly while the remaining balance keeps compounding. Retirement payouts can stay nominal or rise with inflation to preserve purchasing power. Withdrawal tax is used to show the spendable amount after tax. Inflation-adjusted value shows what the future balance is worth in today’s money.
